

Objective :

Insights: 

	1) Women are more likely to buy compaired to men (65%).
	2) Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 States (35%).
	3) Adult age group (30-40 yrs) is max contributing (50%).
	4)Amazon, Flipcart and Myntra channels are max contributing (80%).



Conclusion :

	1) Target women customers of age group (30 - 40 yrs) living in Maharashtra,
Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers/coupons availabe on Amazon,Flipkart
and Myntra.
